Definitions:
Electrowinning- process of recovering metals from solutions, such as dissolved ores, by using electrolysis to deposit the pure metal onto an electrode (Merriam-Webster).

Precipitation – a chemical reaction in solution resulting in the formation of a solid product. From ISO 23044:2020.
